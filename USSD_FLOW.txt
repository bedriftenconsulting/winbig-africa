BACKGROUND PAYMENT FLOW (after customer enters MoMo PIN)
---------------------------------------------------------
1. Customer enters PIN on phone — Hubtel processes MoMo payment
2. Hubtel calls POST /payment/webhook on our server
3. Tickets updated: payment_status=completed, paid_at set, payment_reference (MoMo TX ID) stored
4. One transaction pushed to payment_service (SUM of unit_price across all tickets for the payment_ref)
5. SMS retry worker (runs every 2 minutes via systemd) detects sms_sent=FALSE and sends SMS via mNotify:

   1-Day Pass (1 ACCESS_PASS + 1 DRAW_ENTRY) — 1 SMS:
     CarPark payment confirmed!
     Pass: CP-ACC-XXXXXXXX
     Valid: Day 1 — Sat 3 May 2026
     WinBig Entry: WB-ENT-XXXXXXXX
     Draw: 03 May 2026

   2-Day Pass (2 ACCESS_PASS + 2 DRAW_ENTRY) — 2 SMS (one per day):
     SMS 1:
       CarPark payment confirmed!
       Pass: CP-ACC-XXXXXXXX
       Valid: Day 1 (02/05/2026)
       WinBig Entry: WB-ENT-XXXXXXXX
       Draw: 03 May 2026

     SMS 2:
       CarPark payment confirmed!
       Pass: CP-ACC-YYYYYYYY
       Valid: Day 2 (03/05/2026)
       WinBig Entry: WB-ENT-YYYYYYYY
       Draw: 03 May 2026

   Extra WinBig (DRAW_ENTRY only) — chunked 5 per SMS:
     CarPark payment confirmed!
     WinBig Entries:
     WB-ENT-XXXXXXXX
     WB-ENT-YYYYYYYY
     ...
     Draw: 03 May 2026

6. sms_sent=TRUE set on all tickets for the payment_ref once all SMS delivered
